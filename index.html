Who We Are

D00msDay is a cybersecurity-focused Capture The Flag (CTF) and Hackathon team formed by students enrolled in BSc (Hons) Cyber Security and Ethical Hacking at Coventry University UK.

The team was founded to explore offensive security, digital forensics, open-source intelligence, artificial intelligence, tool development, and cybersecurity research through practical competitions and collaborative projects.

The team maintains a mysterious identity and focuses on skill, innovation, teamwork, and technical excellence rather than individual recognition.

10 April 2026

Formation

D00msDay was officially formed at Coventry University UK.

Mission

Advance cybersecurity skills through competitions, research, collaboration, and practical learning.

Vision

Become an internationally recognized cybersecurity team contributing to research, education, and open-source security tools.
